But data-based improvements will help busses truly thrive. hero_lede_1: Often, Route 27 - Los Gatos’ only bus route - seems resigned to be the anemic southern outpost of the VTA system. VTA continuously cuts back on investment in Los Gatos, first by cutting two routes in 2020, then by downgrading weekend service in 2023, due to low ridership. But VTA still punches above its weight — and latent demand exists that, if unlocked, could reverse this transit death spiral of low ridership and reduced returns. hero_lede_2: In collaboration with the **Traffic Safety Ad-Hoc** of the Los Gatos Youth Commission, LG Think Tank ran a federally prescribed cost-benefit analysis to determine the real impact of Route 27. Even the **current** network, flawed as it is, already returns **$1.71 in benefits for every $1 spent** over 20 years. Travel-time savings, crash reduction, fewer emissions, health benefits from walking to stops, the option value to people who do not ride today but might tomorrow, all of it has a defined methodology under [FTA cost-benefit guidelines](analysis.html#glossary) and [USDOT BCA Guidance 2024](analysis.html#glossary).
